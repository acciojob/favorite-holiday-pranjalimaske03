The Khindsi Lake


Khindsi Lake is a lake near the city of Ramtek in the Nagpur district of India. Boating, watersports, restaurant and resort is operated by Rajkamal Tourism [1] and Olive Resorts at Khindsi lake. It is Central India's largest boating center and amusement park with many tourists visiting every year.
